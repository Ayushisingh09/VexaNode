Frequently Asked Questions

What is Vexanode? +
Vexanode is a premium hosting provider for Discord bots, Lavalink audio nodes, and VPS servers — built by developers, for developers. We run AMD Ryzen 9 and Intel Xeon infrastructure across India, USA, Singapore, and Germany with enterprise DDoS protection on every plan.
Is Discord bot hosting really free forever? +
Yes. Our free tier includes 512MB DDR4 RAM, 1GB NVMe SSD, Intel Xeon Gold CPU, and permanent 24/7 uptime with no sleep mode. No credit card required, no time limit.
Which programming languages are supported? +
Node.js (discord.js), Python (discord.py / Pycord), Java (JDA / Javacord), TypeScript, Go, and Bun — on all plans including free.
Do you have servers in India? +
Yes. Our India node is in Mumbai with sub-20ms latency for Indian users. We also have nodes in USA (Ashburn), Singapore, and Germany (Frankfurt).
What DDoS protection do you use? +
We use a three-layer stack — OVH VAC for L3/L4 network-level absorption (up to 1Tbps), NeoProtect for real-time traffic fingerprinting, and Cloudflare WAF for L7 application-layer filtering. All plans include DDoS protection at no extra cost.
