Free Public Lavalink Nodes

Start developing your Discord music bot instantly. We provide entirely free, public Lavalink endpoints for the community to test and build.

Public Node Fair Usage Rules

These nodes are shared resources provided free of charge to the developer community. To keep them online and stable for everyone, strictly adhere to the following:

  • No 24/7 24/7 Looping: Do not use these nodes to run permanent 24/7 radio bots or "lofi" streams. These nodes are for standard user-commanded playback only.
  • Connection Limits: Maximum of 20 concurrent voice connections per Discord Bot Token.
  • Rate Limits Apply: Search endpoints are heavily rate-limited to prevent YouTube IP bans. Cache your search results on your bot.
  • No SLA: Public nodes may be restarted, updated, or wiped without prior notice. Do not use for large production bots.
