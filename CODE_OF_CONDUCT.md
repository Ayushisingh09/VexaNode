# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making VexaNode a welcoming and respectful community for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in all discussions
- Give and receive feedback professionally
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community
- Help others when you can

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it by contacting the maintainers via the [VexaNode Discord](https://discord.gg/vexanode).

All reports will be handled with confidentiality and reviewed promptly.

## Enforcement

Maintainers have the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who violate this Code of Conduct.
