Free Hosting FAQ

Why is it free? What's the catch? +
There is no catch. We provide free hosting as a service to the developer community. We subsidize the cost of free nodes through our premium enterprise VPS and specialized hosting plans.
Will you put my bot to sleep if it's inactive? +
Absolutely not. Unlike Replit or Glitch, we do not monitor activity to pause containers. Your bot stays online 24/7.
Do I need a credit card? +
No credit card is required to claim the free tier. Simply create an account, verify your email/Discord, and your node is instantly deployed.
Can I run a music bot on the free tier? +
You can host the bot logic, but 512MB RAM is generally not enough to process audio streams directly. For music bots, we recommend connecting your free bot to our Free Public Lavalink nodes.
How do I verify to keep it free? +
To prevent abuse, we require users to join our Discord server. As long as you remain in the community, your free node stays active forever.
