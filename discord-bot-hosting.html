Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I exceed my RAM limit? +
Our system will automatically attempt to safely restart your bot if it runs out of memory. There are no overage charges. If your bot consistently needs more RAM, you should upgrade to a larger plan.
Can I use databases like MongoDB or Redis? +
Yes. You can use external database URIs in your bot code. If you need to host the database itself on our platform, you will need to order a VPS plan, as our Bot Hosting plans are optimized for application processes.
How do I access my files? +
You can use our integrated web-based File Manager, connect via SFTP using clients like FileZilla, or pull your code directly from a Git repository using the startup configuration.
Do you enforce sleep modes like other free hosts? +
No. We absolutely never put your bot to sleep. Your server container runs 24/7/365 regardless of activity, even on the free tier.
How do I upgrade my plan? +
You can instantly upgrade your plan at any time through the billing panel. The upgrade process is seamless, requires no file migration, and applies your new resources immediately upon payment.
